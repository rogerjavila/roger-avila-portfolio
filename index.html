Roger Avila is an independent designer and developer based in Kansas City with a service focus for the creative and hospitality industries

I'm a lover for wine, food, music, art, and basically anything that requires a talent for creativity. My career began in the hospitality industry, well it really began working as a super for different types of real estate maintenance companies taking old interior and remodeling as well as maintenance. My eager learning of different trades has taught me an appreciation of different people's work and the energy their work requires. This allows me to really relate with my clients and really see their visions. Another key to my success has been putting "aces in places." What this means is I'm here to make sure my clients are set up for success based on their needs by putting all the right things together. My experiences are in graphic designing, web design, and development with the use of html, css, and GIT.
